Amazon



Amazon is an American multinational technology company that was founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos. It started as an online marketplace for books, but quickly expanded to include a wide range of products and services. Today, Amazon is one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world, with operations in more than 200 countries and territories.

Amazon's product offerings include books, electronics, clothing, groceries, household items, and much more. They also offer digital services such as Amazon Prime Video, Amazon Music, and Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Amazon has a reputation for providing excellent customer service, with features such as fast shipping, easy returns, and 24/7 customer support. They have also been a pioneer in online retail, with innovations such as one-click purchasing and personalized product recommendations.

Snapdeal



Snapdeal is an Indian social commerce startup that provides an online marketplace for small businesses and individuals to sell their products. However, as a buyer, you can also use the to purchase products from these sellers.

Each product listing includes details such as product images, descriptions, pricing, and reviews from other buyers. You can also use the search bar to find specific products or filter your search results based on your preferences.

Once you find a product you're interested in, you can add it to your cart and proceed to checkout. Snapdeal provides multiple payment options, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. The also provides tracking information so that you can keep track of your order and receive updates on delivery.

Flipkart


Flipkart is an Indian e-commerce company founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. It operates as an online marketplace for a wide range of products, including electronics, clothing, home appliances, and more.

Each product listing includes details such as product images, descriptions, pricing, and ratings from other buyers. You can also use the search bar to find specific products or filter your search results based on your preferences.

Flipkart has gained popularity in India due to its wide range of products, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery. It offers a convenient way to shop for products online and has a reputation for providing excellent customer service.

1mg


1mg is an Indian online healthcare platform founded in 2015 by Prashant Tandon, Gaurav Agarwal, and Vikas Chauhan. It provides a wide range of healthcare products and services, including medicines, lab tests, doctor consultations, and health advice.

Customers can search for medicines and healthcare products using the search bar, browse through different categories, and filter their search results based on various parameters such as price, brand, and availability. 1mg also offers information on various health conditions, their symptoms, and possible treatments.

1mg has gained popularity in India due to its focus on healthcare and its ability to provide a wide range of services under one platform. It offers a convenient way to access healthcare services online and has a reputation for providing reliable and accurate information to its customers.
